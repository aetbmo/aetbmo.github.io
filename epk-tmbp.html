The Most Beautiful Planet | EPK

The Saturn Farmhouse is a trip back in time to melodic songs and electric guitars of a bygone era.

While the vastness of space is on the surface of the band’s calling, The Saturn Farmhouse use this lens to focus back in on everyday existence. Love, lust and even the rise of isolationism have all been addressed in the space based narrative of the band’s debut eps material.

The four piece band is a combination of multi instrumentalists which helps craft the bands classic sound. The ability to swap and share ideas on a variety of instruments creates a harmony within the group that is a core foundation of their music.

In the past two years, The Saturn Farmhouse have been building momentum in the Oxfordshire music scene performing at such established venues as: The Bullingdon, The Wheatsheaf and Fat Lils. This momentum has resulted in the Saturn Farmhouse signing to the MAS development scene with internationally recognised artists Ricky Wilson of The Kaiser Chiefs being involved as a patron.

With the release of the band’s debut EP coming soon the band hopes to use this new material to push their presence to a new and wider audience and to bring guitar music back into the public consciousness.
